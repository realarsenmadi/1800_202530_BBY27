Frequently Asked Questions

What is SureSpot?

SureSpot is a parking assistance app designed to help you easily find available parking spots in your area.

Do I need an account to use SureSpot?

No. You can continue as a guest, but creating an account allows you to save preferences and parking history.

How does SureSpot find parking spots?

SureSpot uses real-time location services to show the most accurate availability near you.

Does SureSpot work in all cities?

Currently it only covers parking lot P22 at the Burnaby BCIT campus. We hope to expand our coverage soon!

Is my data secure?

Yes. We prioritize user privacy and never share your personal information with third parties.

Who created SureSpot?

SureSpot was developed by the BCIT COMP 1800 BB27 team as part of a course project.
